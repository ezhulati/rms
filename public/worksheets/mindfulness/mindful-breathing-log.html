Mindful Breathing Log

This log is designed to help you track your daily mindful breathing practice and observe its effects on your mental and physical well-being. Regular mindful breathing can help reduce stress, improve focus, and promote emotional regulation.

2. After each session, record the details in the daily log table.

3. At the end of each week, reflect on your overall experience and note any patterns or changes.

Common Mindful Breathing Techniques

  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7 counts, exhale for 8 counts
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts
  • Diaphragmatic Breathing: Deep breathing that engages the diaphragm (belly breathing)
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing: Alternating breath between left and right nostrils
  • Counted Breathing: Simply counting your breaths (typically from 1 to 10, then starting over)

Tips for Consistent Practice

  • Practice at the same time each day to build a habit
  • Start with just 5 minutes and gradually increase your time
  • Use a gentle timer to keep track of your practice duration
  • Find a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed
  • If you miss a day, don't be discouraged - simply resume the next day
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you
